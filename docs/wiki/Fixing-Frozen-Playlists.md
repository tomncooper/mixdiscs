## What Does "Frozen" Mean?

When you update your Spotify playlist and it goes over 80 minutes, Mixdiscs shows the last valid version (before it exceeded the limit) instead of removing it entirely.

## How to Fix

**Simple**: Just remove tracks from your Spotify playlist until it's under 80 minutes.

1. Click the "üîó Remote" link on the frozen playlist
2. Remove enough tracks to get under 80:00
3. Wait for the next site update (happens automatically)
4. Your playlist will unfreeze automatically

**No PR needed!** The fix happens on Spotify, not GitHub.

## Prevention

When adding tracks to your remote playlists:
- Check the total duration on Spotify
- Keep a 2-3 minute buffer below 80:00
- Remember: updates sync automatically!

## FAQ

**Q: Will my playlist be deleted?**  
A: No, it stays visible using the cached version.

**Q: How long do I have to fix it?**  
A: No time limit. Fix it whenever you want.

**Q: Can manual playlists freeze?**  
A: No, only remote playlists can freeze because they auto-sync.
